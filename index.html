What is Scorigami?

Scorigami is a concept popularized by Jon Bois. It is a scoreline (e.g. 104-95) that has never occurred before in league history.

Because NBA scores cluster around 95-115 points, scores in that range are common, while extremely high, low, or asymmetrical scores are rare and create new Scorigami points.
